Air Writing

About

What is Air Writing?

Air Writing is a technique that allows individuals to write or draw in the air, without the need for pen and paper or any physical surface. It is a form of gestural communication that utilizes motion sensors and computer vision technology to track the movements of the user's hand or fingers and convert them into digital images or text.

Why do we need Air Writing?

Hearing-impaired people face significant communication barriers, particularly when interacting with hearing individuals who do not know sign language. This can lead to feelings of isolation and exclusion. This project provides an effective way of communication using air writing and it enables users to convey many messages with little effort. Air writing is often more easily understood than other forms of communication, such as lip reading, which can be ambiguous or unclear.
